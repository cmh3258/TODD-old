TODD

To Do and Discover

Share events, places, food and so much more with friends, family and everything inbetween

Imagine Pinterest but with lists of things to do in your city as well as the city you are traveling too

Integrated with Foursquare, Eventbrite, TripAdvisor, Facebook, Yelp and Eventful

TODD puts everything happening in your city, all the best restaurants and sites to visit and much more on one app. Allowing you to have one place to find things to do. You can keep track of your bucket list for your city, share it with friends, plan trips with your family and whatever you can think of.

Never forget the cool spots you haven't been too. Never forget which restaurant you have been wanting to try.

The website that wants you to get off your computer and go explore the world with the wonderful people who make it what it is.
